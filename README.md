# Strand - Portable Gig Reputation & Credit Protocol

> Your wallet is your resume. Your earnings history is your credit history.

## What Is Strand?
Strand helps gig workers turn verified work history into a portable on-chain reputation and credit profile.

Workers can upload earnings proofs, build a transparent Strand Score, and unlock fairer access to credit without starting from zero on every platform.

## Why Strand Exists
Gig workers earn consistently, but their trust data is fragmented across apps.

- Reputation is trapped inside each marketplace.
- Workers switching platforms lose proof of reliability.
- Traditional credit systems miss real earning behavior.

Strand solves this by making work history portable, verifiable, and usable for lending decisions.

## Who Strand Is For
- Gig workers (delivery, rideshare, and similar roles) who want portable credibility.
- Lenders and fintechs who need better borrower signals than static credit checks.
- Marketplaces and partners who want trusted worker identity primitives.

## What You Can Do With Strand
- Create verifiable work records from earnings history.
- Build a dynamic Strand Score (0-1000).
- Share a public worker reputation profile.
- Access score-based credit pathways.

## How To Use Strand (Deployed App)
Use the live app here: [https://strandapp.vercel.app/](https://strandapp.vercel.app/)

1. Open the app and connect your wallet.
2. Upload your earnings proof in the worker dashboard.
3. Let Strand process and verify the record.
4. Check your updated Strand Score and profile.
5. Explore available credit options based on score eligibility.

## Product Flow
```text
Connect Wallet -> Upload Earnings Proof -> Verify Record -> Update Strand Score -> Unlock Credit Access
```

## Why It Matters
- Portable trust: your reputation follows you across platforms.
- Better financial inclusion: work consistency becomes credit context.
- Transparent scoring: clear, trackable reputation signals.
- Open protocol mindset: reusable reputation primitive for ecosystem integrations.
